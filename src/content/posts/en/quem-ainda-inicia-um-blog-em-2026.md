Who still starts a tech blog in 2026?

Me, apparently.

This isn't my first blog. Actually, I've lost count of how many I started — and abandoned — over the years. Wordpress, Blogspot, you name it... I'd always begin with enthusiasm, publish for a while, and then life would absorb my time and the blog would become yet another idle domain.

So why again? And why now?

## What changed

The short answer: **AI**.

I'm not talking about using AI to write posts (although it helps with reviewing, translating, and organizing). I'm talking about the fact that, in 2026, an AI agent helped me **build the entire blog** much faster.

Stack of choice: Astro + Tailwind + Cloudflare Workers. Multilingual (PT/EN/ES). Simple light/dark mode. Automatic deploys via GitHub. Monthly cost: $0.

It wasn't me sitting down coding everything from scratch. It was me talking to my agent, defining the architecture, and it implementing while I focused on what matters: the content.

## Who am I

My name is **Vinicius Buscacio**. I'm a Cloud Solution Architect at Microsoft Brazil, focused on Microsoft Teams and AI applied to customers. I worked many years as a Cisco Consultant, and deployed several Cisco Call Managers out there.

Outside of work, I spend time on personal projects — some became real products, others stayed as learning experiences. I recently published a technical book about Microsoft Teams on Amazon (3 languages), experimented with countless AI agents until I found one that fit 100% of what I needed (I'll talk about it in the future, obviously).

That's what I'll write about here: **cloud, AI, automation, and the projects I'm building.**

## What to expect

- Technical posts about tools and technologies I'm using day to day
- Personal projects documented — from concept to production

I promise 100% biased and opinionated takes on the cloud and AI market.

## Will it be different this time?

Honestly? I don't know. But what I can say is that this time the cost to maintain is zero, deploys are automatic, and I have an AI agent that will hold me accountable if I stop writing.

Welcome to `viniciusbuscacio.blog`. I hope you stick around.
